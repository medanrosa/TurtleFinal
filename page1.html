Welcome to the Turtle Hatcheries in Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is home to five species of sea turtles that visit its shores to nest throughout the year.

Unfortunately, these turtles are threatened by various factors such as coastal development, poaching, and pollution.

To protect these amazing creatures, the government of Sri Lanka has established several turtle hatcheries along its coastline.

Turtle Population up to date --- 1,133,000 Turtles

What We Do

At our turtle hatcheries ..........................

1. we collect and incubate turtle eggs until they hatch, and then release the baby turtles into the ocean.

2. We also rescue and rehabilitate injured turtles, and conduct research on their biology and behavior.

3. We work closely with local communities to promote sustainable tourism and conservation practices.
